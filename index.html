Travel to Bindoy

Bindoy is a municipality town located in the east side of the island of Negros. The municipality of Bindoy is a two-hour drive north from Dumaguete City, the capital of Negros Oriental, Its total area covers 173.1 kmÂ². The province of Negros Oriental is located in the central region of the Philippines. This municipality has a tropical climate that experiences a fair amount of rainfall through the wet season with hot and humid air having an average temperature of 26 degrees Celsius. During summer time it can be very hot and dry. The best solution against the heat is to jump into the refreshing ocean and snorkel at the nearby Mantalip Reef.

Bindoy Government Officials

Mayor Eniego C. Jabagat

Jabagat, Eniego Calidguid is a candidate running in the 2022 Philippine elections for Municipal Mayor. He is seeking to be elected to a seat in Bindoy (Payabon), Negros Oriental. Jabagat is running for office under the Nacionalista Party banner. Jabagat will appear on the ballot as "1 JABAGAT, ENIEGO (NP)".

Vice Mayor Louie Rastica

Louie Rastica is running for office as a Municipal Vice-Mayor in the Philippines' 2022 elections. He is running as a candidate for the Nacionalista Party (NP). He is hoping to be elected to the seat in Bindoy (Payabon), Negros Oriental. On the ballot, he will appear under the name "2 RASTICA, LOUIE (NP)".

Representative ocelyn Limkaichong

Jocelyn Limkaichong is a candidate running for member of the House of Representatives in the upcoming Philippine elections. She is running under the Liberal Party ticket and is targeting the First District. She is seeking to be elected to the post in Negros Oriental Province. She will be on the ballot as "1 LIMKAICHONG, JOSY (LP) on May 9, 2022.
